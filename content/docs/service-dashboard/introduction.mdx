# ANC Service Dashboard

Live at [services.ancsports.net](https://services.ancsports.net) (internal: `abc-anc-services.izcgmb.easypanel.host`).

The Service Dashboard is where the service side of ANC lives. Everything after a deal is won and installed — keeping the venue running, staffing events, handling tickets, tracking workflow — lives here.

![Service Dashboard overview](/img/screenshots/service-dashboard/01-dashboard.png)

## Who uses it

| Role | What they do here |
|------|------|
| **Admins** (leadership) | Configure roles, venues, services, assignment rules |
| **Tech Support leads** (Chris and team) | Triage tickets, assign technicians, merge duplicates, run reports |
| **Operations managers** (Joe and team) | Staff events, monitor workflows, track labor budgets |
| **Technicians** | See assigned events, check in on site, submit game-ready + post-game reports |
| **Clients (venue-side)** | Report issues via the portal, view live workflow status |

## What it does, at a high level

- **Event management** — every game, concert, or service event at every venue
- **Staff assignments** — who works what event, workload visibility, labor budget tracking
- **Venue management** — 200+ venues, their Slack channels, contracted services, installed screens
- **Ticket system** — issue tracking with SLA, auto-assignment, merge for duplicates, bulk close
- **Voicemail → ticket** — the Zoom support phone line automatically opens tickets with the recording, transcript, and caller number attached
- **Workflow tracking** — check-in → game ready → post-game report, with reminders at each stage
- **Client portal** — per-venue page where clients can see status and open tickets
- **Reports** — SLA compliance, coverage rate, labor hours, workflow completion
- **AI diagnostic** — paste a symptom, get a knowledge-base-backed troubleshooting plan

## Relationship to the rest of ANC

- **Proposal Engine** (proposals.anc.com) is where the venue was sold. When a project goes live, the venue, screens, and client record flow in here automatically.
- **Twenty CRM** (crm.ancsports.net) is the system of record. Every ticket, event status change, and workflow step pushes into Twenty in real time — so the CRM always reflects the operational state.
