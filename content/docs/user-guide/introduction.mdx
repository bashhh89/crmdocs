## What the CRM is

The ANC CRM is a custom-built account-management and proposal-tracking system that replaces the prior Salesforce org. It lives at:

- **Production:** [crm.ancsports.net](https://crm.ancsports.net)

It tracks:

| What | How many |
|---|---|
| Companies | 3,730 |
| People | 19,966 |
| Opportunities | 8,360 |
| Revenue splits | 5,160 |
| Service tickets | 3,900 |
| Design requests | 20,174 |
| Venues | 374 |

## Who uses it

| Role | Primary use |
|---|---|
| **Executive leadership** | ANC 2026 Company Dashboard, account-level revenue visibility |
| **Proposal Lead** | Estimation & Proposals view, Backlog, Win/Loss by League |
| **Services** | Proposal Pipeline, Bid Tracker |
| **Finance & Ops** | Payments, Backlog, contract status |
| **VP Services** | Linked tickets at the account level (day-to-day operations stay on the Service Dashboard) |

## The three-vertical model

Revenue rolls up into three verticals:

| Vertical | CRM value | What's inside |
|---|---|---|
| **Technology** | `TECHNOLOGY` | LED displays, LiveSync hardware/software, portables, products |
| **Venue Services** | `VENUE_SERVICES` | Service contracts, maintenance, field operations |
| **Media & Sponsorship** | `MEDIA_SPONSORSHIP` | Ad sales, graphics, content production, feeds |

Salesforce used 5 RecordTypes that mapped into these three — the CRM does the roll-up automatically.

## What's different from Salesforce

- **One workspace, not two apps.** Executive dashboards and proposal reports live together.
- **Account-centric.** Every ticket, opportunity, person, venue, estimate, and design request surfaces on the Company detail page.
- **AI built in.** The assistant answers questions like *"margin on Knicks deals this year"* directly from the sidebar — no SQL, no report builder.
- **Customization without add-on cost.** Changes ship from the internal team, not through third-party professional services.
