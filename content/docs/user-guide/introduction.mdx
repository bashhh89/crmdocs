## What the CRM is

The ANC CRM is a **custom-built Twenty instance** that replaces our Salesforce org. It lives at:

- **Production:** [crm.ancsports.net](https://crm.ancsports.net)
- **Alternate:** [abc-twenty.izcgmb.easypanel.host](https://abc-twenty.izcgmb.easypanel.host)

It tracks:

| What | How many |
|---|---|
| Companies | 3,730 |
| People | 19,966 |
| Opportunities | 8,360 |
| Revenue splits | 5,160 |
| Service tickets | 3,900 |
| Design requests | 20,174 |
| Venues | 374 |

## The three-vertical model

Everything revenue-related rolls up into three verticals:

| Vertical | Twenty value | What's inside |
|---|---|---|
| **Technology** | `TECHNOLOGY` | LED displays, LiveSync hardware/software, portables, products |
| **Venue Services** | `VENUE_SERVICES` | Service contracts, maintenance, field operations |
| **Media & Sponsorship** | `MEDIA_SPONSORSHIP` | Ad sales, graphics, content production, feeds |

Salesforce had 5 RecordTypes that mapped to these 3 — the CRM does the roll-up for you.

## What's different from Salesforce

- **One CRM, not two apps.** Jireh's dashboard and Natalia's reports live in the same workspace.
- **Account-centric.** Every ticket, opportunity, person, venue, estimate, and design request surfaces on the Company detail page.
- **AI built-in.** Boyka (our custom agent) can answer "what's the margin on the Knicks deals this year" in the sidebar without SQL.
- **No per-seat add-on cost.** Customization doesn't go through Salesforce professional services — it ships from our side.
